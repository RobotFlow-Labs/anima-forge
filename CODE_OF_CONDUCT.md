# Code of Conduct

We are committed to a welcoming, harassment-free community for everyone, regardless of
background, identity, experience, or ability. Be respectful, assume good intent, accept
constructive feedback, and focus disagreement on technical ideas rather than people.

Harassment, threats, discriminatory language, unwanted sexual attention, deliberate
intimidation, and publishing another person's private information are unacceptable.
Maintainers may remove content or restrict participation when behavior harms the
community.

Report conduct issues privately through the repository's GitHub security advisory
interface. Reports will be reviewed promptly and handled as confidentially as possible.

This policy is based on the Contributor Covenant 2.1.
